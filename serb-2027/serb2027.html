Welcome to the School

The first Experimental High Energy Physics school was held in Jan 1995. This SERB-sponsored school has trained generations of PhD students. The 2019 edition at TIFR brought together leading experts, hardware workshops, and advanced software training.

📄 School Schedule (PDF)

Eligibility

Research scholars, post-docs, faculty, and motivated MSc/MTech students. Prerequisites: Particle physics, matter interactions, and C++ programming.

About the School

This year the school is held at Tata Institute of Fundamental Research, Mumbai from January ??, 2027. The program includes intensive classroom lectures, software exercises with ROOT, Geant4, and hands-on hardware experiments with detectors, FPGA, and silicon photomultipliers.
