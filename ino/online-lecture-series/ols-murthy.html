About the Artist
Prof. M.V.N. Murthy had his initial training in Carnatic music under the tutelage of his sister, Ms. Amba Ramamurthy and later under Vidwan Dr. Vijayraghavan in Mysore.
He was trained in Hindustani Music by the great Guru, late Pandit Krishnand of Kirana Gharana.
Prof. Murthy is one of few musicians in India who plays classical Hindustani music with the Saraswati veena.
He has performed at many prestigious venues and festivals in India and abroad.
Professionally, he is a theoretical physicist, and a Fellow of the Indian Academy of Sciences (Bengaluru).
