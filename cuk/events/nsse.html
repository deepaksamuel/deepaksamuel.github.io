National Seminar on Science and Science Education

Azim Premji University in association with Central University of Karnataka

About the seminar...

Azim Premji University has been organizing Seminars in Indian languages on subjects related to school education in collaboration with different Universities across India. In the last two years, two seminars in Kannada have been held in collaboration with various Universities across Karnataka. As a part of the seminar series, during 2021, a joint seminar is planned to be organized in collaboration with Central University of Karnataka with the theme "Science and Science Education".

The proceedings of the seminar will be in Kannada. In order to participate in the seminar, interested participants need to submit their written paper in Kannada. People whose papers are accepted by the expert panel will be invited to attend the seminar.

Loding and food during all the seminar days, for all the participants, will be covered by the organizers. TA for all the participants will also be reimbursed by the organizers (Conditions apply).

Tentative date of programme 11-13 December 2021
Last date for submission of abstracts and registration 20 August 2021
Confirmation of acceptance 30 August 2021
Last date for submission of full paper 25 October 2021
Venue Central University of Karnataka, Kalaburagi
